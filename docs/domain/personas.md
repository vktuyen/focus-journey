# Personas

Who uses Vietnam Focus Journey, and what "good" looks like for them.

## The focused individual — *primary (v1)*
- **Role / context:** developer, student, or remote worker doing long solo focus sessions.
- **Goals:** stay motivated through deep work; feel a sense of progress without gamified pressure.
- **Frustrations:** productivity trackers feel like surveillance and kill trust before they help.
- **Signals of success:** sustained focus sessions; the journey advances and is worth glancing at.

## The privacy-skeptical teammate — *primary (v1)*
- **Role / context:** a peer who won't install anything that could be monitoring them.
- **Goals:** verify the trust claim before adopting.
- **Frustrations:** vague privacy promises that don't match what the software actually reads.
- **Signals of success:** onboarding privacy claims provably match real API usage (`/privacy-audit` passes).
